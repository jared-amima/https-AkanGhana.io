NAMING BIRTH SYSTEM IN AKAN

Description

Akan is a Ghanian type of system used to give names to children and also calculate there birthday regarding there gender based basis.corresponding to there birthdays.Akanians names are derived from Ghanian Culture.Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first names as a 'day Name'which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.
